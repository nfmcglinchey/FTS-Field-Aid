Market Visit Checklist

Part 1: Pre-Visit

Salesforce Account Review

  • Check the account hierarchy, call history, and contacts.
  • Look under the parent account in the related tab for specific customer goals.
  • Understand the business objectives that the customer aims to achieve.
  • Identify active promotions for the customer in Salesforce under the related tab in enrollment locations.

Part 2: District Manager (DM) Meeting

Create Pre-Cap for DM

  • Craft personalized pre-visit communications addressed to the DM emphasizing training goals and requesting a meeting.

Plan for the DM Meeting

  • Schedule a meeting with the DM to discuss strategies and address potential objections.
  • Possible topics to cover during the meeting:
    • Introduction and welcome.
    • Overview of the visit purpose and goals.
    • Training session plans.
    • Current market snapshot using data analytics.
    • Proposed strategies for improvement.

Part 3: Objection Handling

  • Anticipate potential objections and prepare persuasive responses.

Part 4: Location Visit

  • Provide targeted training based on VIA/SAC SF findings and DM meeting insights.
  • Topics to cover:
    • Sales strategies and best practices.
    • Product knowledge and promotion execution techniques.

Part 5: Post-Visit

  • Update Salesforce with information gathered during the visit.
  • Send recap email summarizing key points and offering ongoing support.
  • Hold a post-visit debriefing with the account manager to discuss training effectiveness and areas for improvement.
  • Discuss and order any POS or equipment needed.
