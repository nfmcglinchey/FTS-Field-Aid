My To-Do List

    Market Visit Checklist ▶

    Part 1: Pre-Visit ▶

    Salesforce Account Review

    • Check account hierarchy, call history, and contacts.
    • Look under parent account for specific customer goals.
    • Understand the business objectives the customer aims to achieve.
    • Identify active promotions for the customer in Salesforce.

    Performance Analytics

    • Use VIA or SAC tools to identify successes and opportunities.
    • Note relevant data on sales, services sold, premium percentages, etc.

    Define Training Objectives

    • Define 2-3 training objectives for each location visit.

    Part 2: District Manager (DM) Meeting ▶

    Create Pre-Cap for DM

    • Craft personalized pre-visit communications for training goals.

    Plan for the DM Meeting

    • Schedule a meeting to discuss strategies and objections.
    • Possible topics to cover:
      • Introduction and welcome.
      • Overview of the visit purpose and goals.
      • Training session plans.
      • Current market snapshot (analytics).
      • Proposed strategies for improvement.

    Part 3: Objection Handling ▶

    • Anticipate objections and prepare persuasive responses.

    Part 4: Location Visit ▶

    • Provide targeted training based on VIA/SAC data and DM insights.
    • Topics to cover:
      • Sales strategies and best practices.
      • Product knowledge and promotion execution.

    Part 5: Post-Visit ▶

    • Update Salesforce (immediately after each visit).
    • Send recap email summarizing key points and support.
    • Debrief with account manager on training effectiveness.
    • Discuss and order any needed POS or equipment.
